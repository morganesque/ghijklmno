House Pic

— 2nd July 2002

After the over-whelming number of requests I’ve had, I’ve decided to put up a picture of the new house. I’t s a semi so our bit is the part closest to you (this side of the white door). Isn’t it nice?

Anyway, cunning spotter Kev has noticed that if you look carefully you can actually see a hand in the doorway. That is me putting a new Yale lock on the front door. I had to chisel a large part of the door frame away in order to fit it - so that’s me mid-chisel. I only mention this because if any of you are thinking "aha now I know where he lives I’ll go and rob him", you’ll be sorely dissapointed when you come up against that Yale lock my friend - it’s absolutely enigmatic in it’s power and complexity!!
