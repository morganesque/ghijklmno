Beach Snaps

— 2nd February 2003

There’s some new photo’s here from the weekend before last when we went back to Formby to visit the parents and had a nice walk on the beach. I’ve been meaning to go back and take some pictures of all this for a while now coz it looks great and it’s where I grew up and so I’m proud that it looks great etc etc. Luckily we had a beautiful clear wintery day which was perfect for picture taking. My favourite is this one of the sun coming through the trees, it’s got that blue circle of glare which kind of makes it look cool.
