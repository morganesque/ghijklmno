Now List

— 27th March 2003

A few things happening at the moment.

  • Monday and Tuesday I was off with a sickness. It’s gone mostly but what remains is mainly in my throat.
  • Kirsty’s Grandad on her fathers side died earlier this week. Kirsty is going to the funeral tomorrow.
  • There’s a great big fucking war on and the politicians are starting to say things like "Hello, it looks a bit more complicated than we thought, you won’t mind us killing a lot of Iraqis after all will you? Oh and can we have some more cash?"
  • Bought myself some nice new trainers at the weekend. I haven’t bought myself nice new trainers for ages. I used to revel in the activity. I took me back.
and that’s about it.
