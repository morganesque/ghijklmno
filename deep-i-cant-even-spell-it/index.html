Maybe that’s it. Maybe you’re not being payed for your skills or for your ability but you’re actually being payed just to make the journey every morning and to stay in one place for 8 hours a day. In exchange for the money you require to live and luxuriate you’re agreeing not to do anything more worth while with your life. It’s not selling your soul, it’s not that pro-active.
