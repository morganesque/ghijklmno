Long Waits

— 2nd May 2003

Very happy today. I’ve eventually got round to ripping all my Tom Waits albums and writing the resultant mp3 files onto a CD. I now have a whole CD dedicated to Mr Waits’s album sized output (bar a few stragglers) which I’ve brought into a work, inserted into one big winamp playlist and am now enjoying, one randomly chosen track after another.

And thus the "high psychic price" I pay for working here is offset slightly.
