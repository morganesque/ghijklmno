At Last

— 20th November 2002

Well, it’s been a while in planning and I’ve managed to manage it pretty badly indeed, so basically there it is. No big fan fare nothing really in the way of a celebration. Not even a small free gift for each of you. I’ve reached my 100th week of drawing drawings for this website and I’ve also, during the same period, completed the grand total of one hundred drawings. Bringing my per-week totally finally back up to an average of one. Long may it stay that way (fat chance to be honest).

The ticker-tape parade will be held next Tuesday starting at Morrisons in Guiseley and ... well ... pretty much wandering home. I don’t imagine there’ll be much in the way of ticker, but I think I might still have some old tape around here somewhere.
