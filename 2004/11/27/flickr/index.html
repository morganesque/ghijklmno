Flickr

— 27th November 2004

Over there on the right. I’ve added a little column of images that represents the latest uploads to this online photo management service Flickr, which I’ve been getting into recently.

No doubt it’ll only turn out to be a passing fad or something but for the time being I’m going to leave it over there and see how it goes. I’m going to try and remember to take some interesting pictures with the camera in my phone, so maybe it’ll be a good way to keep this thing supplied with new content.

Also, you may notice that I’ve updated the design slightly. I just started tinkering and didn’t stop. Oh well, that’ll keep it going for another few months.
