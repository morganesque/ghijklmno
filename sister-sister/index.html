Sister Sister

— 29th October 2002

Woo hoo! My little sister is coming to stay at the weekend. She hasn’t seen the house yet, in fact she’s the last relation of either mine of Kirsty’s not to have (excluding grandparents and cousins and lost long uncles) so I’m dead excited to show her around and everything. She’s driving down from Edinburgh on Saturday with her boyfriend (who’s job pays all his petrol costs!?!?!).

Then next week we’ve got Kirsty’s little sister staying, so she can attend a weeks worth of work experience in sunny Leeds. Buying fashion or something (doesn’t sound like work to me!).

Typical, you wait around for ages for one sister and then two show up in quick succession.
