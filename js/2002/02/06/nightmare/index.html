Nightmare

— 6th February 2002

I had a horrible dream last night that a Freddie Krueger type character was chasing me while reciting endless poetry. It was very disturbing as I was kind of aware I was dreaming and I found myself thinking "What a great idea for a film". The dream ended when the Freddie character turned into Jaws (from the 007 films) and saying something like “more power to my fingers” began to shoot me.
